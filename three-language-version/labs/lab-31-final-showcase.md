## Part 1: Capstone Presentations

### Presentation Format (8-10 minutes each)

1. **Introduction** (1 min)
   - What did you build?
   - Why did you choose this project?

2. **Live Demo** (3-4 min)
   - Show the main functionality
   - Highlight interesting features
   - Handle a few use cases

3. **Technical Deep Dive** (2-3 min)
   - What paradigms did you use and why?
   - Show a piece of code you're proud of
   - What data structures/algorithms were key?

4. **Journey** (2 min)
   - What was the biggest challenge?
   - What would you do differently?
   - What are you most proud of?

### Presentation Tips

**Do:**
- Practice your demo beforehand
- Have a backup plan if live demo fails
- Show enthusiasm for your work
- Be honest about challenges

**Don't:**
- Apologize for your project
- Read code line by line
- Rush through the demo
- Downplay your achievements
